# Changelog

## 0.1.0 | 10 May 2026

- Adds the basic stone well to the wooden build desk, costing 4 flitch and 4 stone.
- Mod settings menu (F1) labels follow your in-game language
- Adds the pump well to the wooden build desk, costing 8 flitch, 4 nails, and 2 iron details. Spawns a pump on the closest existing stone well, so you can pump as many wells as you've built.
- Both wells can be removed via the build desk's Remove button, returning half the build cost.
- The pump well respects the vanilla Engineer tech requirement by default. There's a config toggle to remove that gate if you'd rather have both wells available from the start.
- Localized in 11 languages.
