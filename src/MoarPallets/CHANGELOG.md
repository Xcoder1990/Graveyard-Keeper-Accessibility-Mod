# Changelog

## 0.1.0 | 16 May 2026

- Adds the pallet to the wooden build desk and the cellar build desk, costing 6 flitch and 4 nails.
- Mod settings menu (F1) labels follow your in-game language
- Pallets can be removed via the build desk's Remove button, returning half the build cost.
- The cellar elevator and barn porter now reach pallets anywhere in the cellar, not just next to the elevator.
- Pallets placed in the barn add merchant trading capacity. Anywhere else they work as extra single-slot containers.
- Loose crates auto-route onto the nearest pallet with space, both at game load and when you set one down while carrying. Leftovers when every pallet is full park at the cellar elevator.
- Loose crates stack on the spot instead of drifting off the map.
- Crate crafts pause when no pallet has space, so crates don't get dumped on the floor mid-craft.
- A small "Linked to elevator" pop-up confirms each cellar pallet you build is wired into the elevator and the porter.
- Localized in 11 languages.
